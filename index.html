Ellen Rose

About

I'm a multidisciplinary experience designer and transformation consultant.

My approach combines design-thinking methodologies with elevated visual craft and a high emotional intelligence.

I'm supercharged with AI tools and workflows, yet I believe that the creative process remains a profoundly human practice and must be protected. In fact, are there ways that AI can actually elevate our human creativity and imagination? I'm trying to figure that out.

I use design in my role at SYPartners to inspire individual epiphanies and collective transformation. I'm a New Yorker but now call Portland, OR home.
