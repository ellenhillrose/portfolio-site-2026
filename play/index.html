Once upon a time I designed and installed window displays for iconic New York City retailers like Anthropologie, Saks Fifth Avenue, and MoMA Design Store.

I was the Creative Services Manager for window designs, globally, at J. Crew corporate headquarters before I went back to grad school to reposition into experience design and creative strategy.

I make ceramics.

I paint in acrylics and watercolors. Mostly abstract patterns.

My 1884 Victorian home (along with my family and I) were featured on the show "Diary of an Old Home" on HBO Max and Disney+
